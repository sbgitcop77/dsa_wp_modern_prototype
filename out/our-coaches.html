Meet Our Coaches

Elite Trainers, Proven Results. Our staff brings collegiate and professional experience to every session.

Chris Ford

Owner & Founder

Former Division I Catcher & Professional Player. Chris grew up in Maryland and became a standout catching prospect, earning recognition as the nation's No. 272-ranked catcher. He played Division I baseball at Norfolk State University, where he maintained a .986 fielding percentage and 33% caught-stealing rate over two seasons. He later transferred to Rogers State University (Division II), leading the conference in defensive catching categories in 2019 with a .987 fielding percentage. Chris signed professionally with the White Sands Pupfish and competed with other professional teams, earning MLB workout invitations from organizations including the Boston Red Sox and Detroit Tigers. He founded The Baseball Doctor in 2022 and expanded to launch The Diamond Sports Academy in 2025.

Syeed Mahdi

Hitting & Fielding Instructor

Former Division I and Division II standout. Mahdi's senior season at Shepherd University earned First Team All-MEC honors and Second Team D2CCA All-Atlantic Region recognition. He appeared in 47 games with a .341 batting average, 13 home runs, and 50 RBIs. Previously at Norfolk State University (2015–2018), he earned multiple MEAC All-Academic awards. A four-year high school varsity letterwinner, he hit .400 as a senior and earned Team Maryland MVP honors.

Connor Hax

Baseball Instructor

Former NCAA Division I athlete at UMBC with over five years of coaching experience. Connor has worked at The Diamond for two and a half years, teaching athletes of all skill levels. He previously provided two years of private instruction and served as camp instructor and coordinator at UMBC for three years. He also spent three years at Athletic Performance Inc. coaching players ages 13–15, emphasizing fundamentals and game preparation.

Alison Guckin

Softball Instructor

2× All-MEAC outfielder and Norfolk State softball player (2019–2023). Alison holds the NSU single-season stolen base record and finished 11th in NCAA Division I for stolen bases. She played in Australia in 2023 and currently serves as assistant coach for MD Integrity 13u. Her coaching journey began in 2022 when her cousin requested lessons.

Kody Hines

Baseball Coach

Experienced baseball coach with 16+ years of coaching and private instruction experience. A four-year varsity starter at Archbishop Spalding High School, Kody continued playing at Anne Arundel Community College and Frostburg State University, where he also served as assistant coach. He has coached at multiple respected programs including Spalding, AACC, Frostburg, St. Mary's, and Broadneck High Schools.

Richard Cosgrove

Player Development Coach

Professional coach entering his fifth season with the Baltimore Orioles player development and sixth year in professional baseball. He began with the San Diego Padres in 2021 and was part of the 2023 Triple-A National Champion Norfolk Tides. A three-year varsity starter, he earned Anne Arundel All-County selection and MSABC Futures All-Star honors. Early coaching roles included Phoenixville High School and Lackawanna College.

Jenny Parsons

Elite Softball Coach

Hall of Fame NCAA athlete with 30+ years of experience. A three-sport Anne Arundel County legend earning First-Team All-County and Capital Gazette Player of the Year recognition in volleyball, basketball, and softball. At East Carolina University, she held pitching records including 1.52 ERA, 969.1 innings pitched, 102 wins, and 34 shutouts. She coached 20+ years at collegiate level (10 seasons at Nicholls, 14 at Central Arkansas). In 2023, she led UCA to 45 wins, earning ASUN Coach of the Year and NCAA Mid-Major Coach of the Year honors.

John Dopson

Pitching Coach

Former Major League Baseball pitcher who played for the Montreal Expos and Boston Red Sox during the late 1980s and early 1990s. Born in Baltimore, Dopson debuted in 1985 and joined the Red Sox in 1988, where he became a key rotation member. His 1989 season included over 180 innings pitched. He appeared in more than 100 MLB games and now provides instruction based on professional-level pitching expertise.
